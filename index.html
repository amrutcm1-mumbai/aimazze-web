Professional personas

The Vault
Upload any document — PDF, DOCX, CSV, images. Local vector embeddings mean semantic search runs entirely on your machine. Attach documents to any chat. Nothing uploaded to a server.

Bring your own keys
Connect Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, Groq, Mistral and more. API calls go directly from your device to the provider. AIMAZZE is never in the data path. Your keys are stored in OS Keychain, never in plaintext.

Built with Tauri — not Electron. The entire app bundle is ~8MB vs 150MB for Electron apps. Blazing fast startup, lower memory, and full offline capability via local open-source models.
